# Head of Information Security at Vertiseit Group

## Role Overview

Responsible for leading and strengthening the group's information security, building upon previous experience with security matters from a prior role as a software developer within the company.

## Key Responsibilities

### Strategic Security Leadership

- **Develop and implement** Vertiseit's overall information security strategy
- **Ensure compliance** with relevant standards and frameworks (e.g., ISO 27001)
- **Lead SOC 2 Type 2 program** across Vertiseit Group (Security, Availability, Confidentiality)
- **Drive strategic security initiatives** to continuously improve security posture
- **Responsible for risk management**, incident preparedness, and promoting a strong security culture
- **Collaborate with teams** across the group to integrate security into our processes and systems

### Technical Security Management

- **Build and maintain** our Information Security Management System (ISMS)
- **Conduct risk assessments** and manage compliance requirements
- **Ensure technical safeguards** are in place and functioning properly
- **Oversee security operations** and incident response capabilities

### Cross-functional Collaboration

- **Work closely with CTO** and technical teams to integrate security into development processes
- **Collaborate with legal and compliance** teams on regulatory requirements
- **Partner with HR** to promote security awareness and culture
- **Engage with business units** to understand and address security needs
