# Head of Information Security at Vertiseit Group

## Role Overview

Responsible for leading and strengthening the group's information security across governance, compliance, technical controls, customer trust, and day-to-day security operations.

The role builds on my earlier work as a software developer at Vertiseit, which gives me a practical bridge between policy, engineering, product teams, and customer-facing security requirements.

## Key Responsibilities

### Strategic Security Leadership

- **Lead and maintain** Vertiseit's information security strategy and ISMS
- **Drive compliance programs** across ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and TISAX readiness
- **Translate controls into workflows** that engineering, product, HR, IT, and leadership teams can actually follow
- **Report security posture and control status** to leadership with practical material for decisions and follow-up
- **Build security culture** through training, policy adoption, phishing awareness, and daily advisory work

### Technical Security Management

- **Rebuilt the Information Security Management System** from the ground up
- **Implemented evidence and control workflows** in Vanta for ISO 27001, SOC 2, and future framework work
- **Introduced secure development rules** for developers, including code review and vulnerability review workflows
- **Supported remediation** of vulnerabilities and customer-facing security issues across product environments
- **Improved identity and access controls** through access reviews, inactive-user cleanup, guest cleanup, and SSO work
- **Strengthened endpoint and Microsoft security posture** through Intune compliance and Defender-related work
- **Helped secure AI-assisted development** and internal AI application usage through technical guidance and authentication patterns

### Cross-functional Collaboration

- **Work closely with CTO** and technical teams to integrate security into development processes
- **Collaborate with legal and compliance** teams on regulatory requirements
- **Partner with HR** to promote security awareness and culture
- **Engage with business units** to understand and address security needs

## Selected First-Year Impact

- Led the work around ISO 27001 certification and continued the follow-through into SOC 2 Type II and TISAX readiness.
- Set up security control tracking, evidence collection, and audit workflows for the organization.
- Implemented or improved security policies, policy acknowledgement, and organization-wide security training.
- Evaluated and procured tools for cyber security awareness and phishing simulation.
- Coordinated background screening processes for new employees.
- Ran vendor risk work, including 40+ vendor assessments.
- Led large access control reviews for systems that needed clearer ownership and visibility.
- Established backup coverage and coordinated the organization's first backup tests.
- Created and ran incident tabletop exercises, including templates and organization-wide participation.
- Supported active threat response and follow-up when issues fell outside normal managed SOC coverage.
- Answered customer security questions across multiple products and supported procurement processes with major customers.
- Migrated and consolidated thousands of repositories into GitHub from Azure DevOps, GitLab, Bitbucket, and other GitHub environments.
- Migrated and configured status pages so customers can monitor availability and SLA-related status.
- Helped define and implement AI governance, including secure ChatGPT setup, AI application review, and guidance for safer internal AI adoption.
