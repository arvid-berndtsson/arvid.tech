Doorlook is an AI assistant that helps users look up employers and get employee perspectives on companies. It uses large language models to summarize public information and provide a general view of what employees think about an organization.

## What is Doorlook?

Doorlook is a wrapper around modern AI models (initially GPT‑4o) that asks structured questions about a company and produces a concise overview.

## Future Development Plans

### Platforms and Services

The plan is to provide insight from a variety of sources, when feasible:

- Newspaper articles
- Glassdoor
- Indeed
- Comparably
- LinkedIn posts
- Public blogs
- Union or worker council pages

### AI Models

Future support may include additional models such as Ollama 3.1 and Claude 3.5 for broader coverage and better availability.
