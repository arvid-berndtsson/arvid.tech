## About LimeTip

LimeTip was born from my passion for technology and a deep understanding of the challenges businesses face with VAT compliance. As someone who has been captivated by programming since childhood, I founded LimeTip to provide a reliable and efficient solution for validating VAT numbers across multiple countries.

## Our Mission

At LimeTip, we aim to make VAT compliance simple and accurate. Our powerful VAT validation API ensures that your financial operations run smoothly, freeing you up to focus on what you do best.

## Key Features

**Real-Time Validation**: Instantly verify VAT numbers across multiple countries, ensuring compliance and accuracy.

**Easy Integration**: Seamlessly integrate our VAT validation API into your existing systems with comprehensive and standardized documentation.

**Reliability**: Built on a robust and scalable infrastructure, LimeTip offers consistent and dependable VAT validation.

**Continuous Support**: Our dedicated team provides ongoing support to keep your validation processes running smoothly.
